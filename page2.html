Limitation

1. Scalability Issues

DAM systems often struggle with the rapid growth of digital assets. Quantum computing faces hardware limits that make scaling a challenge.

2. Integration Challenges

Integrating DAM with other systems like CMS or CRM is complex and expensive. Quantum systems need to work with classical computers to overcome their limits.

3. Uncertainty in Quantum Systems

Quantum computers operate on probabilities instead of fixed outcomes, causing difficulties in precise financial tasks and regulatory reporting.

4. Regulation and Security

Centralized DAM systems can fall prey to cyberattacks. Quantum computing may threaten current encryption, raising security concerns.
